About Me

My Story

A software tester's journey — from fundamentals to full-system QA leadership.

"In the realm of software testing, being a skilled tester is more challenging than being a good developer." — John D. McGregor

I'm a Software Tester with more than 10 years of experience in manual testing and recent hands-on experience in automation and performance testing.

My testing expertise spans Black/White Box testing, System Testing, SIT, CCB Testing, POS and E-Commerce testing, Mobile testing, and Automation Testing using UFT and Selenium — with full system development lifecycle experience including designing, developing, and implementing test plans, test cases, and test processes.

The name BUGZ LIFE symbolizes my personal journey in this field — discovering bugs, understanding systems, and documenting them with precision using modern testing tools.
